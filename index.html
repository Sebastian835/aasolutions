OUR STORY

A&A Solutions LLC is a locally operated company led by Diego Maldonado. With six years of hands-on experience in home improvement and property services, our company focuses on providing dependable solutions for homeowners and businesses.

We are committed to delivering professional work with attention to detail and a strong focus on customer satisfaction. Every project we undertake is treated with responsibility, efficiency, and care.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide reliable remodeling, maintenance, and property improvement services that enhance the comfort, safety, and value of every property we serve. We aim to deliver professional results while maintaining high standards of quality and customer satisfaction.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to become a trusted property improvement company in Connecticut, recognized for reliable service, quality workmanship, and strong client relationships.
